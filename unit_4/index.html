POLITISIT

Increasing political diversity through childcare

Everyone should be able to impact their community. Political action, whether this means going to a city council meeting, attending a rally ro running for office, is an important way of creating impact. But anyone with kids can tell you that the cost of childcare can make these actions prohibitive.

Politisit is here to help. We provide money for child care so that monthers, fathers, grandparents, or other child care providers can become involved without having to worry abou their kids. Just send us a request, and we'll work with you to help pay for the child care of your choice.

What kind of activities can be included? Here's a brief list:

  • Attending a school board meeting
  • Speaking at a City Council meeting
  • Attending a rally
  • Running for local or national office

HOW IT WORKS

Simply fill out this form at least 72 hours in advance of your start time. You'll need to have the name and location of the event, how much you will be paying your sitter or child care professional per hour, and how many hours, including your transportation time, you will need to pay your sitter. We will review your request and approve it based on current funds and opporutnity. We are currently able to fund up to 20 hours a month per household.

Need more help? Let us know the circumstance and we'll see if we're able to help.
