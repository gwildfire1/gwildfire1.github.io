Increasing political diversity through childcare

Simple solution. Stronger community.

MISSION

We provide scholarship funds toward child care so that mothers, fathers, grandparents, and other child care providers can become politically involved without having to worry about the cost and care of their children.

HOW IT WORKS

  1. Fill out this form with event information at least 72 hours before event
  2. Set up time with the babysitter of your choice
  3. Upon approval, we will send funds through Paypal or via mail so you can pay your sitter!
